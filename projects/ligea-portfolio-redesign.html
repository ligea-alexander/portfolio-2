My Portfolio Redesign

Redesigning my portfolio to showcase interactive storytelling, thoughtful design direction, and front-end development skills.

Live Site ↗
Focus/Domain Web Design & Development Interactive Storytelling UI/UX Design
Roles Designer Developer Researcher
Duration 3 months (July 2025 - September 2025)
Tools Figma HTML/CSS, JavaScript (GSAP) GitHub Copilot Playwright

Why This Story

My previous portfolio didn't reflect where I am today as a creative technologist. It was static, visually flat, and didn't show my capabilities in animation, interaction design, or narrative case studies. I needed a portfolio that felt alive — professional yet playful — and that clearly positioned me in the space between data, design, and development.


My previous portfolio didn’t reflect the kind of work I do now. It was static, minimal, and lacked the interactive qualities I value in design. I wanted a site that would demonstrate my ability to craft experiences — not just display projects. The challenge was to create a portfolio that felt alive and personal while staying professional, lightweight, and easy to maintain.
