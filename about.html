About Me

Hi I'm Ligea Alexander — a creative technologist and visual storyteller working at the intersection of data, design, and narrative. I craft interactive data stories, lightweight tools, and visual systems that clarify complexity, deepen understanding, and inspire action.

My practice bridges insight, interaction, and impact. I use the visual grammar of information — the patterns, structures, and rhythms that shape how we see and understand — to design experiences that resonate with audiences, shift perspectives, and spark curiosity.

Whether building a data-rich dashboard, an editorial narrative, or an exploratory prototype, I move between mediums, always anchoring in story and clarity. My work spans UX research, data journalism, and interactive design — blending code, structure, and emotion into systems people can feel and use.

Alongside my work in data and design, I contribute reported features and essays to editorial platforms like Dialogue Magazine, exploring ideas at the intersection of culture, identity, and place.

About Me + AI

AI is the conversation — and I don't ignore it. But I don't center it either. My philosophy is simple: AI-integrated, not AI-led. I use AI as a tool to enhance, accelerate, and test ideas — not to direct my voice, taste, or vision.
